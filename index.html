Welcome to ISKCON Hadapsar

ISKCON Hadapsar is a prominent spiritual center in Pune, dedicated to propagating the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita and spreading the holy names of Lord Krishna. Our temple serves as a sanctuary for spiritual seekers, offering a peaceful environment for meditation, kirtan, and profound Vedic learning.

Under the guidance of our Founder-Acharya A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, we invite you to experience the bliss of pure devotion, participate in joyful festivals, and discover the true purpose of human life.
